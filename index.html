In a fiew words

Lamamos is a project dedicated to one mission : give cytizen the tools they need to properly defend there privacy on the internet. For that we are developping an operating systeme installable and usable by anyone, that deploy your own cloud servers. Indeed servers with an s. We think that self hosting is the only way we may garanty our privacy, but we don't want to sacrifice the quality of service. Au contraire we want to improove by giving you the ability to controle all the parametters of thoses services.

Features

  • Redundancy in your servers : if one fail the other one will do the job at it's place.
  • Based on free softwares : you know whath is runing on your machines.
  • You are your own service provider : the only one who can give away informations about yourself is you.
